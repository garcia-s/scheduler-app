We want the user to be able to do this actions:

1) Create a service with the validation of the service integrity
2) Add Workspaces and ServiceProviders to the service
3) Create appointments based on the available schedule for ServiceProviders and Workspaces
4) Get Appointments for a specific client
5) Get a general schedule of the available times for a service
6) Get a list of services with their corresponding ServiceProviders and Workspaces
7) Get a list of appointments for a specific ServiceProvider
8) *Optional* Create consecutive appointments for multiple services
